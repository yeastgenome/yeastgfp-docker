setting up pdp++

L. Gerke - 1/2003

(Successful with Red Hat 7.3). I'm using pdp++2.2


get the bins from: ftp://cnbc.cmu.edu/pub/pdp++/
pdp++_2.2_bin_LINUXstat.tar.gz
pdp++_2.2_ext.tar.gz

tar -xzf pdp++_2.2_bin_LINUXstat.tar.gz
tar -xzf pdp++_2.2_ext.tar.gz
su
mv pdp++ /usr/local
cd /usr/local/bin
ln -s /usr/local/pdp++/bin/LINUXstat/* .
