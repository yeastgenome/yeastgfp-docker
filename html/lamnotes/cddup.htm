CD Duplication

L. Gerke - 1/2003

(Successful with Red Hat 7.3).



cat /dev/cdrom > myimage.iso

md5sum myimage.iso > myimage.md5

cat /dev/cdrom | md5sum > myimageFromCD.md5

diff myimage.md5 myimageFromCD.md5

cdrecord -scanbus # do this to get the device name like "0,0,0"

cdrecord dev=<0,0,0> speed=12 [blank=fast /*for CD-RW */] -v -eject myimage.iso
