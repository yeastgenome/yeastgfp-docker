Manual IP Setup

L. Gerke - 1/2003

(Successful with Red Hat 7.3).



The pertinent files are in:

/etc/sysconfig/network-scripts/ifcfg-eth0 reads
DEVICE=eth0
BOOTPROTO=static
BROADCAST=169.230.13.255
IPADDR=169.230.13.64
NETMASK=255.255.255.0
NETWORK=169.230.13.0
ONBOOT=yes


/etc/sysconfig/network reads:
NETWORKING=yes
HOSTNAME=gerke-oshea
GATEWAY=169.230.13.1


/etc/resolv.conf reads:
nameserver 169.230.18.10
nameserver 169.230.18.20
